Computer Science 1-2 for beginners or intermediate
No experience needed!
Grade Levels: 9th - 12th
Prerequisites: None
Homework: None, unless incomplete classwork

Salary: Computer Science offers high wages, and it's easy to get a starting job

Course Description: Learn to write your own computer games and code a simple web page to showcase your work. Some students complete the starter lessons very early and use the second half of the year to study cyber security or other languages. We start with JavaScript , HTML and CSS, and students can move on and select any language they want: Python, JavaScript, Unity or more. Students are given a lot of time for digital design and art creation. Computer Science is a career that pays very well, remains in high demand, and allows a lot of career choices. Every industry in the world needs people to write apps and web pages.

Student Workload: No homework. Students who enjoy puzzles and problem solving tend to love this class. There is some group work but this class is graded on individual work.
